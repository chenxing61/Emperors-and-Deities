Cultural Diversity
There are 3 levels of cultural diversity:

Low:
Each Player Usually start from a low diversity culture because they are centered around certain majorly historically relevant civilization. 
Like Egypt for North Africa. Powerful from the start. But not really very flexible.

Medium:
Going for medium means more civs from that region is avaliable. Especially those which are still really repersentitive and influencial without being there from the start. Balanced in terms of the adaptability and strength of the uniques.
Like Persia for West Asia

High:
Going for high cultural diversity means even the most niche stuff are going for it. Maxiumum adaptability, but way less powerful.
Like Early Slavic Culture for European
