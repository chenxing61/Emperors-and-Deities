Nomad Civ Structure


Starting out{

    1.Animal Husbendry Unlocked

    2.Have Primitive nomad instead of Hunter-Gatherer. Which only build camps and pastures.

}
Special Units{

    Civil
    1.Primitive Nomads:
    Unit that can fight,building camps and special pastures, but they produce food on tiles.

    2.Nomad Settlers:
    A cheaper settler.

    2.Nomad:
    Unit that is essentially a more mounted version of P.Nomads, can also build roads.

    3.Peons:
    Unit that is captured through war and can build farms,mines and quarry. But they work like civ 6.

    Army (All the generic mercenary are also there. Water unit requires alliance with city states, or special projects.)

    4.Nomad Warriors:
    Replaces Most of the Levy Units, Extra movement on open terrains.

    5.Nomad Cavalry:
    First Real Standing Army of the Nomads, a stronger early light cavalry.

    6.Elite Cavalry Archer:
    Stronger Cavalry Archer.

    7.Khan:
    Great General Replacement.Can be bought using influence. Have even more movements.
    However, its combat bonus is lessened.

    8.(Unique)Iron Pangoda:
    Stronger Heavy Cav.

    9.(Unique)Keshik:
    Stronger Elite Cavalry Archer.

    10.HuiHui Trebuchet:
    Mobile Trebuchet that can be packed to gain extra movements or unpacked.

    11.(Unique)Cossacks:
    Gunpowder era cavalry archer.

    12.Nomad Dragoons:
    Granadier with more movements.
}
Special Building/Improvement/Mechanics:{

    1.Can Choose to settle down after reaching medieval era. Which turns you into a normal civ with some outcome building as permanent modifiers.

    2.Only small scale constructions can be built, not many infrastructures or things related to agriculture. Can use peons to hurry up wonder construction.

    3.Cities are capped at 10 population and Nomad units costs populations.

    4.Special Pastures produces herds in resource forms(which share the same limit as migration) and you can use these to replace the default stat buildings in city. Or transfer them to other cities.

    5.Migration, Import and outport costs nothing. And is in a smaller scale.

    6.You can switch capital at zero cost.

    7.You have basic walls and nothing else, so either be a hunter or be hunted.

    8.Horse Related Buildings Have extra effects on army.

    9.Sythians Wagons, Gold working, tombs
}
